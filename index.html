Summer of Science

The Maths and Physics Club of IITB conducts Summer of Science (SoS) every summer in which the participants are expected to study a topic of their choosing under the guidance of a mentor and in the end, write a report on it. I chose to study The Theory of Relativity. I ended up covering most of Special Relativity and just had an overview of General Relativity. You can find my report here.
